SUMMARY: I was getting tired of waking up with a sore neck every morning. My 3 year old 'MyPillow' just wasn't cutting it anymore. Also as a CPAP user, it is hard to get good support and not fight the hose all night long.

I find that I sleep in one spot most all night long. I have been using this pillow now for 3 weeks. The pillow provides perfect support for my neck and keeps me perfectly aligned.

I use the millet side up against my head because it conforms better, cradles my head, and keeps my ear from getting sore if i turn my head during the night. Having the buckwheat on the bottom provides the stability and firmness to keep my head from sinking too low.

### THE_VERDICT
>**FINAL_ANALYSIS:** This pillow definitely earns a spot in the OTD household. The only thing that has kept me from purchasing a pillow like this in the past is the price.
I've gotten too old and have gotten to the point where comfort and a great night's sleep is more important than worry about something as trivial as price when it comes to my health and comfort.
I wish I had purchased this pillow or something just like it years ago.
I don't think anyone would be disappointed in this pillow.
For those of you on a CPAP, I highly recommend this pillow.
